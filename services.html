Home Inspection

Our flagship service provides a complete structural and quality assessment of your flat, apartment or villa. Ideal for ready-to-move properties in Bangalore — before you hand over the full payment or take possession.

What We Check

  • Structural integrity — walls, columns, beams, slabs
  • Plumbing — leakages, water pressure, drainage slopes
  • Electrical — wiring quality, MCB panel, earthing, socket placement
  • Finishing quality — tiles, flooring, paint, woodwork
  • Doors, windows and grills — fit, finish, hardware
  • Kitchen & bathroom fixtures
  • Terrace, balcony, common areas

Re-Inspection

After our initial inspection and you've submitted the defect list to your builder, a re-inspection confirms whether all issues have been genuinely rectified — or just patched up cosmetically.

What We Verify

  • All defects from original report — resolved or not
  • Quality of repairs done by builder
  • Any new issues caused by repair work
  • Builder compliance checklist signed off

Dampness & Seepage Inspection

Bangalore's climate combined with monsoon-season construction shortcuts makes dampness one of the most common and costly defects. We use thermal imaging cameras and moisture meters to detect hidden seepage before it costs you lakhs.

Technology Used

  • FLIR thermal imaging camera
  • Professional digital moisture meter
  • Visual mapping of moisture paths
  • Roof and terrace waterproofing assessment
  • Bathroom and kitchen wet-area seepage detection

Multi-Stage Construction Inspection

If you've booked an under-construction property in Bangalore, this service protects you throughout the build. We monitor quality at each critical stage so defects are caught when they can still be fixed — not after completion.

Inspection Stages

  • Foundation & Slab: concrete mix, reinforcement, curing
  • Brickwork: mortar quality, wall plumb, blockwork
  • Plastering: thickness, finish, cracks
  • Waterproofing: terrace, bathrooms, wet areas
  • Finishing: tiles, paint, woodwork, doors, windows
  • Final Handover: complete snag check

Area Measurement

Many Bangalore builders charge for a super built-up area but deliver less carpet area than promised. Our laser measurement service gives you accurate, documented measurements so you know exactly what you paid for.

What We Measure

  • Carpet area (usable internal area)
  • Built-up area (incl. wall thickness)
  • Super built-up area vs. what was sold
  • Balcony, utility & common area proportions
  • Room-by-room breakdown with floor plan overlay
