Our Story

About Namma HomeCheck

Founded in Bangalore by civil engineers tired of seeing homebuyers get shortchanged by poor construction quality

Why We Started

Bangalore's real estate market is one of India's fastest-growing — and most complex. Every year, thousands of families invest their life savings into apartments and villas, often without a single independent technical check of the property they're buying.

We've seen first-hand what happens: seepage discovered after possession, electrical shortcuts that become fire hazards, area delivered 15% less than what was sold. By the time buyers realise the problem, the builder has moved on and leverage is gone.

Namma HomeCheck was founded to give Bangalore's homebuyers a fair chance. We are certified civil engineers — not salespeople — and our only loyalty is to you, the buyer.
