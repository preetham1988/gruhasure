About Home Inspection

A home inspection is a thorough technical assessment of your property carried out by a certified civil engineer. It covers structural integrity, plumbing, electrical systems, finishing quality, dampness and more. In Bangalore, where construction quality varies widely, an inspection helps you identify defects before you take possession — when you still have leverage to get them fixed by the builder.

Ideally, book an inspection before the final possession — at least 2–3 weeks before your possession date. This gives you time to compile a defect list and present it to the builder before the final payment. For under-construction properties, stage inspections should happen throughout the build.

Under RERA Karnataka, builders are liable for structural defects for 5 years post-possession and are required to deliver the property as per agreement. Our detailed, photo-backed report becomes evidence you can use to demand rectification. Most builders comply when presented with a professional technical report.

Yes — as the buyer, you have every right to bring a technical expert for a pre-possession inspection. Builders in Bangalore are generally cooperative. We have conducted hundreds of inspections across Bangalore without access issues.

Our Process

A standard 2BHK flat inspection takes approximately 2–3 hours. A 3BHK or villa may take 3–4 hours. We never rush — thoroughness is our priority.

You will receive a comprehensive PDF report via email within 24 hours of the inspection. The report includes photos, defect descriptions, location mapping and recommendations. We also provide a WhatsApp-ready summary for ease of sharing with your builder.

We recommend you or a trusted representative be present — it helps you understand the findings in real-time. However, if you can't attend, we conduct the inspection with your authorization and document everything thoroughly in the report.
